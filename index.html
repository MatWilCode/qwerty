Berikut 3 contoh jawaban yang bisa kamu langsung pakai (simple English, sesuai level sekolah): --- **Astronaut/Cosmonaut:** Gherman Titov --- ### **Fact/Event #1:** He was the first person to stay in space for more than one day. During the Vostok 2 mission, he orbited Earth 17 times and spent about 25 hours in space. This proved that humans can live and work in space for a long time. **Source:** “Gherman Titov’s Day-Long Flight Proved Humans Can Live in Space,” Space Age Chronicle [https://spaceagechronicle.com/gherman-titovs-day-long-flight-proved-humans-can-live-in-space/](https://spaceagechronicle.com/gherman-titovs-day-long-flight-proved-humans-can-live-in-space/) --- ### **Fact/Event #2:** He became the first person to experience space sickness. He felt dizzy and sick because of weightlessness. This helped scientists understand how the human body reacts in space. **Source:** “A History of Space Sickness,” National Space Centre [https://www.spacecentre.co.uk/news/space-now-blog/a-history-of-space-sickness-how-to-avoid-throwing-up-when-there-is-no-up/](https://www.spacecentre.co.uk/news/space-now-blog/a-history-of-space-sickness-how-to-avoid-throwing-up-when-there-is-no-up/) --- ### **Fact/Event #3:** He was one of the youngest astronauts ever, flying at only 25 years old. After his mission, he became an important figure in the Soviet space program and helped train other cosmonauts. **Source:** “Gherman Stepanovich Titov,” Encyclopaedia Britannica [https://www.britannica.com/biography/Gherman-Stepanovich-Titov](https://www.britannica.com/biography/Gherman-Stepanovich-Titov) --- Kalau mau, aku bisa bantu lanjut bikin **space blog (POV Titov)** juga 👍

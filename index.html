Explain three reasons that industry developed slowly in the South.** Industry developed slowly in the South because it focused on farming, used slave labor, and had fewer railroads. **2. Why did some Southern leaders want to develop industry in the South?** Some Southern leaders wanted to develop industry because the South depended too much on the North. **3. How did people in the South transport their crops and goods?** People in the South transported their crops and goods by rivers and some railroads. **4. How was the railroad system in the South different from the railroad system in the North?** The railroad system in the South was shorter and not connected. The North had more railroads. **5. How did the railroad system in the South affect the growth of cities?** The railroad system in the South made cities grow more slowly.
